Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Jenga Project Management mobile application ("Jenga PM").

1. Information We Collect

We collect account information (such as name and email), profile image from sign-in providers, and project data you create including milestones, budgets, expenses, diary entries, contacts, files, and other content necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to authenticate users, provide core functionality, synchronize data across devices, improve app reliability and performance, and communicate important service-related updates.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers required to operate the platform, authentication providers, or when required by law or legal process.

4. Third-Party Services

Jenga PM may use third-party services such as authentication providers, analytics tools, and cloud infrastructure providers. These services may process your data strictly on our behalf and in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active or as necessary to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and secure storage practices. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

8. Children's Privacy

Jenga PM is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

10. Contact
